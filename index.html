Food Access Analysis: Camden County, NJ

Authors: Vincent Lu & Ahmad Shahbaz
Course: 34:970:595 Command-Line GIS

1. Data Methodology

Sources: Data for this project was retrieved from the USDA Food Access Research Atlas (2019), the US Census Bureau (ACS 5-Year Estimates, 2015-2019) via the cenpy library, and NJ Transit Open Data[cite: 14, 15, 16].

Processing: Spatial data was processed using GeoPandas. Key geoprocessing steps included a spatial join between NJ Transit bus stop point features and Census Tract polygons to identify transit-rich corridors[cite: 17, 24]. Data was originally in Shapefile and CSV formats.

Data Quality Note (Requirement 3a): To ensure statistical reliability, any Census variables with a Coefficient of Variation ($CV \ge 40$) or missing values have been specifically symbolized. In the maps below, these areas are represented by a grey hatch pattern to distinguish them from low-value data.
